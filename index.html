Objective

The main objective of this project is to achieve localization and multiclass segmentation of coastal debris using state of the art Encoder-Decoder architectures like Segnet and U-Net

Overview

Trash accumulation in beaches affects the ecosystem of coastal lines. Different types of trash can affect beaches in a variety of ways and manual identification of these trash might become laborious. So, it becomes important to devise a method to facilitate the localization of trash without human intervention.

Hence, we propose a deep learning based solution for multi-class segmentation of trash objects using Unmanned Aerial Vehicles. But the problem with the aggregated orthoimages is the low foreground-to-background ratio which tends to render very high false positives (FP) during classification. In order to counter this, we propose two methods namely Randomly Placed Object (RPO) method and Trash Cluster Generator (TCG) to generate synthetic data over real backgrounds.
